Curriculum Group · Author Pods

Bake better activities, together.

Pods of authors meeting online to help each other build Doenet activities.

Two flavors · join as many as you like

Proofing Room

Silent working group

Quiet, protected time to build your own activities - with built-in accountability.

No cap on size - any number of authors can work alongside each other.
Say a quick hello, then work side by side in focused silence.
No agenda, no pressure - just show up and make progress.
Need to talk something through? Slip into a breakout room.

Test Kitchen

Active collaboration

Share what you're cooking up and get pedagogy-first feedback.

Small by design - about three authors per kitchen.
Bring an activity or idea - a soft expectation to have something to show.
Discussion centers on pedagogy - what could work better.
No one brought anything? It simply runs as a proofing room.
A typical session
~10 minCheck-in & community time
12–15 minEach presenter - live demo or a volunteer run-through
FeedbackPresenter's choice: a few quiet minutes of written feedback in the chat, or a round-robin discussion that iterates in real time.

Reviewed activities can move on toward the Curated Library of Activities.
